Is manufacturing shifting from China to India?

As of 26 October 2023

Sovereign: JPM EMBI Global Diversified IG; Corporate: JPM CEMBI Broad Diversified IG

Source: Lazard, JP Morgan

As the United States and European Union increase tariffs on Chinese exports and China becomes more difficult for investors to navigate, companies may be looking to move production away from China to more attractive alternatives. While countries like Mexico, Poland, Vietnam, and Thailand vie for the top spot, India, with its young population and growing economy, might be leading the pack.
